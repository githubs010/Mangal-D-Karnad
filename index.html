About Me

The Author Behind the Stories

Mangal D Karnad's author journey is shaped by a lifelong relationship with words, observation, and lived experience. Her writing spans poems and stories, and children's literature that explore memory, belonging, and everyday life.

"Growing up in Mulki, a small coastal town, taught me that the richest stories aren't invented — they are remembered."

Alongside children's fiction, Mangal writes poems about life examining ambition, ageing, work, relationships, and personal growth — grounded, reflective, and intentionally restrained.

Beyond writing, she is the co-founder of FableSquare and the creative soul behind Yarn Bear.
